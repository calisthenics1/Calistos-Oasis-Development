Who We Are

Building the Foundation of Healthy Communities

Founded in 2014, Calistos Oasis Development is a Ghanaian non-governmental organisation dedicated to addressing the water and sanitation crisis in rural and peri-urban communities. We believe that clean water unlocks education, economic opportunity, and dignity for every person it reaches.

Through community-led design, sustainable engineering, and rigorous hygiene education, we deliver lasting impact — not just infrastructure.

What We Do

Featured Projects

Water Access

Borehole Drilling — Volta North

Installing 28 community boreholes fitted with solar-powered pumps to serve over 8,000 residents in the Volta North Region.

Sanitation

WASH Facilities — Rural Schools

Constructing gender-separated, fully accessible toilet blocks and handwashing stations in 14 primary schools across Western Region.

Education

Hygiene Awareness Campaign

Community-wide training on handwashing, safe water storage, and open-defecation-free practices, reaching 22,000 beneficiaries.
