Why Give

Every Cedi Flows Far

100% of public donations go directly to project delivery. Our operational costs are covered by institutional grants, so your gift is deployed with zero administrative dilution.

💧

GH₵ 50 — Treats a family's water supply for one year

Covers water treatment tablets and education materials for a household of five.

🚿

GH₵ 500 — Installs a handwashing station

A tippy-tap station at a school or clinic, complete with soap and hygiene signage.

☀️

GH₵ 5,000 — Powers a borehole with solar

Solar panel array, submersible pump, and control panel — zero fuel cost forever.

🌍

GH₵ 25,000 — Funds a full community WASH program

From site survey to commissioning, covering drilling, infrastructure, and two-year support.
