Opportunities

How You Can Help

Whether you are an engineer, educator, health worker, communicator, or simply someone who cares — there is a place for you in the Calistos Oasis family.

⚙️ Field Engineers & Technicians
Support borehole drilling, pump installation, and equipment maintenance in the field. Civil, mechanical, and electrical engineers welcome.
📣 Community Outreach Workers
Conduct hygiene awareness sessions, household surveys, and open-defecation-free triggering events in target communities.
🎨 Design & Communications
Create educational materials, social media content, infographics, and annual reports that amplify our message.
💻 Data & Technology
Help build and maintain our water-point monitoring dashboard, mobile data-collection tools, and GIS mapping systems.
