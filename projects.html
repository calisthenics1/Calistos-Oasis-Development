Our Work

Projects & Programs

Six active initiatives, thousands of lives transformed.

Ongoing

Volta North Region

Project Oasis — Borehole Network

Drilling and equipping 28 community boreholes with solar-powered submersible pumps and stainless-steel distribution tanks. Each point is mapped with GPS and linked to a remote monitoring dashboard.

Impact: 8,400 beneficiaries · 28 boreholes
Ongoing

Western Region

Safe Schools WASH Program

Construction of 56 toilet stalls, 14 handwashing stations with tippy taps, and a menstrual hygiene management room in 14 primary and junior high schools.

Impact: 7,000 pupils · 14 schools
Completed

Ashanti Region

Open Defecation Free Initiative

A two-year community-led total sanitation programme that achieved ODF certification in 12 villages through household latrine subsidies, communal triggering events, and follow-up monitoring.

Impact: 12 ODF villages · 6,200 households
Ongoing

Northern Region

SolarPump Retrofit Project

Replacing diesel-powered pumps at 40 existing water points with solar-powered alternatives, cutting operational costs by 80% and enabling 24-hour water supply for the first time.

Impact: 40 sites · 15,000 beneficiaries
Planning

Central Region

Urban WASH Corridor — Mankessim

Designing a piped water distribution network for the Mankessim township to serve 12,000 peri-urban residents currently relying on expensive and unsafe sachet water vendors.

Target: 12,000 beneficiaries · 2026 launch
Completed

Greater Accra Region

Youth Water Ambassadors Program

Training 400 secondary-school students as hygiene advocates who then delivered water-literacy campaigns to over 22,000 community members across 18 districts.

Impact: 400 ambassadors · 22,000 reached
